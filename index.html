Problem and Solution Overview

Many individuals struggle with managing their leisure time effectively, leading to wasted opportunities for relaxation and productivity. Our project aims to provide a solution that helps users balance their recreational activities with productive tasks.

Our platform offers tools to track time spent on leisure activities, set goals for personal hobbies, and optimize time allocation between different interests.

Research and Insights

Through surveys and interviews, we discovered key issues people face with managing their free time:

  • Overwhelming choice of activities, leading to decision fatigue.
  • Poor time-tracking habits for recreational tasks.
  • Difficulty balancing relaxation and productive hobbies.

Key Features

Based on our research, our platform offers the following features:

  • Time tracking for various leisure activities.
  • Recommendations based on personal preferences and past activities.
  • Goal setting for productive and relaxing tasks.

Design Process

Our design process focused on creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface:

  • Clear visual representation of time spent on activities.
  • Simple navigation for adding and tracking new hobbies.
  • Integration with calendar apps to provide real-time suggestions for available leisure slots.
